MY PROBLEM SOLVING PROCESS

Identify

The first step to solving any problem is to identify it as a problem, hopefully while it is still a small one. Often the hard part is seeing past all the smoke and finding the true source of the fire. Once you have done this you can move on to the next step.

Find the solution.

Once you know the cause of the problem, you can begin the process of finding the solution. At this stage of the game it is key to ask lots of questions, gather all the data you can and then use it to begin to find the solution. Once you have gathered the data you can find the best solution, hopefully one that keeps the problem from coming back again.

Communicate the solution

All the knowledge, and solutions, you might have is useless if you cannot communicate it clearly and effectively. This is my favorite part. I enjoy taking the solution and putting in a form that my clients or co-workers can easily understand and use.
